About Us

OUR VISION

To create a society through the help of all of us where the less privilege can strive better, and give educational opportunity to every child irrespective of who they are Our intention is to help eradicate poverty through education. We, consider education as key to poverty alleviation and to national development.

OUR MISSION

To provide educational materials, scholarship for individual children who have a promising academic achievement but may not have means to continue their studies. To ensures every child right to education.
